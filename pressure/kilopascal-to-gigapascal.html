How to Convert Kilopascal to Gigapascal

1 kPa = 1.0E-6 GPa
1 GPa = 1000000 kPa

Example: convert 15 kPa to GPa:
15 kPa = 15 × 1.0E-6 GPa = 1.5E-5 GPa
