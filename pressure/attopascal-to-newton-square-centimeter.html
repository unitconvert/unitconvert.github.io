How to Convert Attopascal to Newton/square Centimeter

1 aPa = 1.0E-22 newton/square centimeter
1 newton/square centimeter = 1.0E+22 aPa

Example: convert 15 aPa to newton/square centimeter:
15 aPa = 15 × 1.0E-22 newton/square centimeter = 1.5E-21 newton/square centimeter
