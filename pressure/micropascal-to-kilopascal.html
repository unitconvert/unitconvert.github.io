How to Convert Micropascal to Kilopascal

1 µPa = 1.0E-9 kPa
1 kPa = 1000000000 µPa

Example: convert 15 µPa to kPa:
15 µPa = 15 × 1.0E-9 kPa = 1.5E-8 kPa
