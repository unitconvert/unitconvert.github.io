How to Convert Exapascal to Kilonewton/square Meter

1 EPa = 1.0E+15 kilonewton/square meter
1 kilonewton/square meter = 1.0E-15 EPa

Example: convert 15 EPa to kilonewton/square meter:
15 EPa = 15 × 1.0E+15 kilonewton/square meter = 1.5E+16 kilonewton/square meter
