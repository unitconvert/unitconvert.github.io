How to Convert Microbar to Femtopascal

1 µbar = 1.0E+14 fPa
1 fPa = 1.0E-14 µbar

Example: convert 15 µbar to fPa:
15 µbar = 15 × 1.0E+14 fPa = 1.5E+15 fPa
