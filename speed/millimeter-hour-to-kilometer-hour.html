How to Convert Millimeter/hour to Kilometer/hour

1 mm/h = 1.0E-6 km/h
1 km/h = 1000000 mm/h

Example: convert 15 mm/h to km/h:
15 mm/h = 15 × 1.0E-6 km/h = 1.5E-5 km/h
