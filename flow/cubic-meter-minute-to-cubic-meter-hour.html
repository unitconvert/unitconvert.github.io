How to Convert Cubic Meter/minute to Cubic Meter/hour

1 cubic meter/minute = 60 m^3/h
1 m^3/h = 0.0166666667 cubic meter/minute

Example: convert 15 cubic meter/minute to m^3/h:
15 cubic meter/minute = 15 × 60 m^3/h = 900 m^3/h
