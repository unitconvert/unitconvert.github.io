How to Convert Megabyte/second to Byte/second

1 MB/s = 1048576 B/s
1 B/s = 9.5367431640625E-7 MB/s

Example: convert 15 MB/s to B/s:
15 MB/s = 15 × 1048576 B/s = 15728640 B/s
