How to Convert Gram/liter to Gram/cubic Meter

1 g/L = 1000 g/m^3
1 g/m^3 = 0.001 g/L

Example: convert 15 g/L to g/m^3:
15 g/L = 15 × 1000 g/m^3 = 15000 g/m^3
