How to Convert Megahertz to Nanohertz

1 MHz = 1.0E+15 nHz
1 nHz = 1.0E-15 MHz

Example: convert 15 MHz to nHz:
15 MHz = 15 × 1.0E+15 nHz = 1.5E+16 nHz
