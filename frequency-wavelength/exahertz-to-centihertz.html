How to Convert Exahertz to Centihertz

1 EHz = 1.0E+20 cHz
1 cHz = 1.0E-20 EHz

Example: convert 15 EHz to cHz:
15 EHz = 15 × 1.0E+20 cHz = 1.5E+21 cHz
