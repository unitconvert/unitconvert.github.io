How to Convert Picohertz to Kilohertz

1 pHz = 1.0E-15 kHz
1 kHz = 1.0E+15 pHz

Example: convert 15 pHz to kHz:
15 pHz = 15 × 1.0E-15 kHz = 1.5E-14 kHz
