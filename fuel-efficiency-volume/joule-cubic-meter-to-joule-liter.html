How to Convert Joule/cubic Meter to Joule/liter

1 J/m^3 = 0.001 J/L
1 J/L = 1000 J/m^3

Example: convert 15 J/m^3 to J/L:
15 J/m^3 = 15 × 0.001 J/L = 0.015 J/L
