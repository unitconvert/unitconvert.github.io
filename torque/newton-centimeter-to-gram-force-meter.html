How to Convert Newton Centimeter to Gram-force Meter

1 N*cm = 1.019716213 gf*m
1 gf*m = 0.980665 N*cm

Example: convert 15 N*cm to gf*m:
15 N*cm = 15 × 1.019716213 gf*m = 15.2957431947 gf*m
