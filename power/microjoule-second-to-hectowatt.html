How to Convert Microjoule/second to Hectowatt

1 µJ/s = 1.0E-8 hW
1 hW = 100000000 µJ/s

Example: convert 15 µJ/s to hW:
15 µJ/s = 15 × 1.0E-8 hW = 1.5E-7 hW
