How to Convert Picowatt to Petajoule/second

1 pW = 1.0E-27 PJ/s
1 PJ/s = 1.0E+27 pW

Example: convert 15 pW to PJ/s:
15 pW = 15 × 1.0E-27 PJ/s = 1.5E-26 PJ/s
