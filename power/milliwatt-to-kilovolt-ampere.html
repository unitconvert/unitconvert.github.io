How to Convert Milliwatt to Kilovolt Ampere

1 mW = 1.0E-6 kV*A
1 kV*A = 1000000 mW

Example: convert 15 mW to kV*A:
15 mW = 15 × 1.0E-6 kV*A = 1.5E-5 kV*A
