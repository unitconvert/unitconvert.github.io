How to Convert Deciliter to Cc

1 dL = 100 cc, cm^3
1 cc, cm^3 = 0.01 dL

Example: convert 15 dL to cc, cm^3:
15 dL = 15 × 100 cc, cm^3 = 1500 cc, cm^3
